Jan Forslow has for the last five years been Product Line Manager for Network Solutions at Itron. This entails managing a fleet of more than 20 million SmartMeter in the United States alone. Prior to joining Itron, Jan held the position as VP Product Manager at Sonim Technologies developing ruggedized cell phones and Public Safety applications. In the early 2000s, Jan was one of the founders of a Mobile-VPN company called ipUnplugged. Jan has a Masters of Electrical Engineering degree from Linkoping University, Sweden, and is currently enrolled in a Master Program on Information Data Science at UC Berkeley!
